## üôã FAQs

### How do I connect to my Commodore 64 Ultimate device?
Ensure your Android device is connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your Commodore 64 Ultimate device. Open the app, and it will automatically discover the device.

### What if the app does not connect?
Check that your Android device and Commodore 64 Ultimate are on the same network. Restart both devices if needed and try reconnecting.

### Can I use this app with other Commodore products?
Currently, C64 Commander is specifically designed for the Commodore 64 Ultimate device.
